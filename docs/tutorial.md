# Tutorial: Your First Ergonomic Keyboard

Welcome to the Ergogen tutorial! This guide will walk you through the process of designing your first ergonomic keyboard from scratch using Ergogen. We'll cover everything from the basic configuration to generating the files for your PCB and case.

This tutorial is inspired by the excellent blog post series by [FlatFootFox](https://flatfootfox.com/ergogen-introduction/), and aims to provide a comprehensive, step-by-step guide for beginners.

## What you will learn

By the end of this tutorial, you will be able to:

*   Understand the basic concepts of Ergogen.
*   Create a complete keyboard layout using a YAML configuration file.
*   Generate PCB and case files for your design.
*   Know what the next steps are to get your keyboard manufactured and assembled.

## Tutorial Parts

This tutorial is divided into several parts. We recommend following them in order.

*   **[Part 1: Units and Points](./tutorial-part-1-units-points.md)**: Learn how to define the basic layout of your keyboard.
*   **[Part 2: Outlines](./tutorial-part-2-outlines.md)**: Create the shape of your keyboard's plate and PCB.
*   **[Part 3: PCBs](./tutorial-part-3-pcbs.md)**: Configure the PCB, including footprints for switches and other components.
*   **[Part 4: Cases](./tutorial-part-4-cases.md)**: Design a 3D-printable case for your keyboard.
*   **[Part 5: Next Steps](./tutorial-part-5-next-steps.md)**: Understand what to do after you've generated your files with Ergogen.

Let's get started!
